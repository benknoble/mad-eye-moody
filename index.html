About

Mad-Eye Moodify is a platform designed to make designing mood-centric playlists based around the music you ACTUALLY LIKE, even easier. Mad-Eye Moodify pull songs from the playlists that you actually listen to, classifies them based on mood, and returns a playlist perfectly tailored to your current mood. Angry, sad, happy, or peaceful, we can help.
